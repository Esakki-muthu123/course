UI/UX Design Course

about

  • User Focus: UI/UX design is all about creating digital products that are easy to use and meet the needs of users.
  • UI vs UX: UI (User Interface) is how a product looks (buttons, colors, layout), while UX (User Experience) is how a product feels and works when you use it.
  • Design Thinking: A creative process where designers understand user needs, come up with ideas, test them, and improve based on feedback.
  • Wireframing and Prototyping: Wireframes are simple sketches of a productâ€™s layout, and prototypes are interactive models to test how it works.
  • Testing: UI/UX designers test their designs with real users to find problems and make improvements, ensuring the product is user-friendly.
